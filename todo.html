Welcome to The Never-Ending List

This is your ultimate to-do list application, designed to help you manage your tasks efficiently. Whether you need to jot down your grocery list, track your assignments, or plan your next big project, The Never-Ending List has got you covered.

Features:

  • Add new tasks with ease.
  • Mark tasks as completed and keep track of your accomplishments.
  • View your outstanding and completed tasks in one place.
  • Simple and intuitive design for seamless navigation.

How to Use:

  1. To add a task, navigate to the Add section.
  2. Enter your task details and click the Add Task button.
  3. To mark a task as completed, go to the Finish section and enter the task's serial number.
  4. Check your progress in the Display section, where you can see both your outstanding and completed tasks.
